# Mission and vision

## Mission

Compress regression archaeology from hours of senior attention into one deterministic artifact.

The job of `faultline` is not to invent new policies. It wraps the predicate the operator already trusts,
walks history safely, and narrows the credible change surface.

## Vision

Every red build should come with a black box recorder.

`faultline` is the history-side counterpart to merge-side gates. It explains where a failure entered history,
what changed around that boundary, and where a human should begin reading.

## Design principles

1. Honest over impressive.
2. Predicate-native.
3. Local-first.
4. Artifact-first.
5. Narrow core, thick edges.
6. Microcrates by seam, not by enthusiasm.
